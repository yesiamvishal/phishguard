    ABOUT THE MODEL

    🧬

    ALGORITHM

    Random Forest Classifier with 200 decision trees, class-balanced weighting, and cross-validated hyperparameters.

    📊

    FEATURES

    3000-dim TF-IDF bigrams combined with 10 hand-crafted features: URL count, suspicious TLDs, urgency words, caps ratio, and more.

    🗂️

    DATASET

    135 curated samples covering real-world phishing patterns: bank fraud, prize scams, account alerts, and corporate safe emails.

    🛡️

    DETECTION

    Identifies suspicious TLDs, urgency language, financial lures, credential harvesting phrases, and IP-based URLs.
